Our Story

The inception of Kôė was to craft a micro-scale coffee roasting and food curation experience, enveloped in a serene ambiance, aiming to deliver a holistic package for our cherished clientele.

Our Vision

Creating a coffee roasting cafe involves a passion for quality, dedication to roasting, and fostering a welcoming space. It includes learning about diverse beans, perfecting roasting, and curating a menu showcasing rich flavors. The cafe prioritizes sustainability, fair trade, and supports Indian coffee farmers.
